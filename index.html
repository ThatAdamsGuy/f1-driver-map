Driver Teammate Network

Interactive graph for current drivers, former teammates, and indirect links.

Select a driver to place them at the centre and arrange everyone else by graph distance.

Legend

Selected

Click a node or link.

CSV format

Nodes: driver_teams.csv with Name,Team Id,Team Name

Links: driver_edges.csv with Driver A,Driver B,Weighting

Layout tips: Structured rings keeps current drivers on the inner ring with outer nodes ordered by who they connect to. Current drivers centred keeps the current grid grouped neatly in the middle. Driver puts one selected driver in the middle and places everyone else into concentric rings by graph distance.

Hover: non-connected and distant nodes fade based on graph distance.
